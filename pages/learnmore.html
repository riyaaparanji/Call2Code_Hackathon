About ClubConnect

Empowering student clubs through modern tools and seamless collaboration.

🎯 Our Mission

We're here to simplify how student clubs function — from event planning to recruitment and analytics. Our goal is to connect students, enhance collaboration, and foster a thriving campus community through innovative technology and intuitive design.

🚀 Key Features

  • 📅 Easy Event Management and Promotion with intelligent scheduling
  • 👥 View all the clubs you've joined at a glance, with quick access to their latest activities and updates.
  • 📝 Streamlined Club Recruitment with automated workflows
  • 📊 View all upcoming events across your enrolled clubs in one unified calendar

💡 Why We Built This

Student clubs are the heart of any campus. Yet managing them is often chaotic and disconnected. This platform was built by passionate students who experienced these challenges firsthand. We created a unified, beautiful, and efficient solution that serves everyone — from casual members to club administrators and university staff.
